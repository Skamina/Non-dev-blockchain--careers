# Narrative Designer

## Overview
Shapes the storytelling, lore, and narrative experience for blockchain products, games, or communities.

## Key Responsibilities
- Develop and maintain consistent narrative and themes.
- Collaborate with creative, product, and marketing teams.
- Write scripts, storylines, and user-facing content.

## Required Skills
- Strong storytelling and creative writing.
- Understanding of game or product narrative structures.
- Ability to translate technical concepts into engaging narratives.

## Example Tasks
- Craft backstories for NFT collections or metaverse assets.
- Write in-app dialogue or tutorial scripts.
- Build narrative-driven campaigns or events.

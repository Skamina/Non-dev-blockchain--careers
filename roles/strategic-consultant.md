# Strategic Consultant

## Overview
Advises on high-level strategies and decision-making for blockchain projects and organizations.

## Key Responsibilities
- Provide market and competitor analysis.
- Guide organizational and product strategy.
- Identify growth and optimization opportunities.

## Required Skills
- Analytical and strategic thinking.
- Excellent presentation and communication skills.
- Industry knowledge in blockchain and technology.

## Example Tasks
- Facilitate strategy workshops.
- Prepare executive reports and recommendations.
- Advise on expansion or pivot opportunities.
